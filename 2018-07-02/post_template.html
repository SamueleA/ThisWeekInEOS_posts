This Week in EOS

2nd of July 2018

Editorial

This week in EOS everyone is talking about RAM speculation. In effect, it turns out people are hoarding RAM in the hopes of selling it at a higher price later. This has caused the price of RAM to skyrocket. It has therefore become expensive for Dapp developers to deploy their dapps and for users to open accounts on EOS. Will the market forces make the price go down by itself or will we need to change how RAM is distributed? This Week in EOS will follow the matter closely and report on the subjet.
