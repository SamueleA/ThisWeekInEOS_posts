Editorial

If there is one thing I can say with absolute confidence about the EOS ecosystem, it is that it's bursting with activity right now. I am seeing so many block producers coming up and assisting the community. We are seeing new projects sprouting every week. The developer community is slowly taking shape. People are discussing how EOS will impact the blockchain space.

It's really hard to predict the future. However, based on all the data I gather for This Week in EOS, it is clear that there is a lot of support for EOS.

It is a great privilege and opportunity to be part of this journey right from the beginning, and I hope it will be for you also!
